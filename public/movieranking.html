The Best Movies According to Inder :)

My top 3 movies of all-time are listed below.

The perks of being a wallflower

Shows vulnerability and the importance of mental health and friendships.

10 things I hate about you

It is a pretty good romantic teen comedy with an enemies to lovers trope

Little Women

Its a story of sisters about stepping into womanhood and going through many ups and downs in life and endeavour to make important decisions that can affect their future.
